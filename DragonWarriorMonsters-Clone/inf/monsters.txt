INTRODUCTION - READING THE MONSTER DATA -------------------------------------
=============================================================================
For those of you wondering exactly where the data came from, all that was
really needed was the ROM (don't ask me for it, I don't have it anymore) and
a hex editor. I then made a program to strip the data from the ROM and place
it in a txt file. I actually wrote this FAQ twice (-.-v) since the first
time it was lost in a hard disk crash before release.

Note that this exact data is also available on my site, in the monster
database, at http://dwm2.no-ip.com/, where it is displayed graphically.

This FAQ can assist one in selecting the exact monster a person wants -- so
long as they know how to read the information. =P This is why this section
is here. Be sure to read the proceeding text as it will tell you exactly
what a lot of the numbers mean.

ML: Maximum Level of the monster, unless it has a + as a result of breeding
EP: Experience growth rate, 0 is the FASTEST growth and 31 is the SLOWEST.

	     ___________________________________________________
	    | Experience Growth Rate:                           |
	  XX|                                                   |
	  XX|    0 ---------------- 15 ---------------- 31      |
	  XX|  Superb            Average              Poor      |
	  XX|   Growth              Growth             Growth   |
	  XX|                                                   |
	  XX ---------------------------------------------------
	  XXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXX

HP, MP, AT, DF, AG, IN: Hit Point, Magic Point, Attack Power, Defense
Power, Agility, and Intelligence growth rates. This is the opposite of EP
growth. 0 is the SLOWEST growth rate, 31 is the FASTEST.

	     ___________________________________________________
	    | HP MP AT DF AG IN Growth Rate:                    |
	  XX|                                                   |
	  XX|    0 ---------------- 15 ---------------- 31      |
	  XX|  Poor              Average              Superb    |
	  XX|   Growth              Growth             Growth   |
	  XX|                                                   |
	  XX ---------------------------------------------------
	  XXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXX

Here's an example:

----------------------------------------------------------------
Name       ML EP HP MP AT DF AG IN SkillA    SkillB    SkillC
----------------------------------------------------------------
Slime      40 16 22 11 14 17 11 13 Firebal   MegaMagic Radiant

A Slime has a maximum base level of 40, an experience growth rate of 16
(Average), an HP growth rate of 22 (Good), an MP growth rate of 11
(Below Average), etc. You get the point? HP MP AT DF AG and IN all follow
the same growth scheme. Higher number = better! =)

0 being the WEAKEST resistance to that group of spells, 3 being the STRONGEST
resistance to that group of spells. The groups are as follows:
